Squamish, BC

The land of epic potential

We're the land of the most densely packed outdoor recreation activities on Earth. Sail your boat to port, climb the two-thousand foot granite cliffs of the Stawamus Chief, let the wind pull you across the marina kiteboarding, bike tour the picturesque route of Squamish to Whistler, ski our deep powder winters, and explore the vast region.

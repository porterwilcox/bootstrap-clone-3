Squamish, BC

The land of epic potential

We're the land of the most densely packed outdoor recreation activities on Earth. Sail your boat to port, climb the two-thousand foot cliffs the Stawamus Chief, propel across the water kiteboarding, bike tour the picturesque route of Squamish to Whislter, ski our deep powder winters, and explore the vast region.
